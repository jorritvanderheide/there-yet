There Yet is a privacy-friendly, proximity-based alarm app. Place a pin on the map, set a radius, and get alerted when you arrive at your destination.

No accounts, no tracking, no Google Play Services required. Your location data stays on your device.

Features:
* Place alarms anywhere on the map
* Set a custom radius for each alarm
* Background monitoring with adaptive GPS polling
* Works offline — map tiles are cached locally
* Search for places with Photon geocoding
* Material You dynamic theming
* Multiple languages supported

There Yet uses OpenStreetMap tiles and runs entirely without proprietary services. It's designed for commuters, travellers, and anyone who wants a simple "wake me up when we get there" alarm without sacrificing privacy.
