Never miss your stop again. There Yet is a privacy-friendly location alarm that wakes you when you arrive. Place a pin on the map, set a radius, and go. Your phone alerts you as soon as you reach the area.

Great for:
* Falling asleep on the bus or train without missing your stop
* Long road trips when you want to rest until you reach town
* Commuting through unfamiliar areas
* Walking or cycling to a new place
* Anyone who wants a simple "wake me up when we get there" alarm

Privacy by design:
* No accounts, no sign-up, no email required
* No tracking, no analytics, no ads
* No Google Play Services
* Your location never leaves your device
* Fully open source under the EUPL 1.2 licence

Features:
* Place multiple proximity alarms anywhere in the world
* Custom radius per alarm, from tight city blocks to wide zones
* Background GPS monitoring with adaptive polling to save battery
* Works offline once the map area is cached
* Search any place with Photon geocoding
* Material You dynamic theming and AMOLED true-black mode
* Available in 14 languages

There Yet uses OpenStreetMap tiles and runs entirely without proprietary services. Built for commuters, travellers, parents with napping kids, and anyone who wants a GPS arrival alarm that respects their privacy.
